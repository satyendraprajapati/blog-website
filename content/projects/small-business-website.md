## Problem

A small business's website was built years earlier on a drag-and-drop site builder. It was slow
to load on mobile, looked inconsistent across pages after years of ad-hoc edits, and every small
content change (a new price, a new service, an updated photo) required paying an outside
developer and waiting days for a turnaround.

## Approach

- Rebuilt the site from scratch as a fast React application with a clean, reusable component
  structure instead of a page-by-page drag-and-drop layout.
- Set up a simple, CMS-free content workflow (structured content files) so the client's own team
  could update text, prices, and images themselves without touching any code.
- Optimized images and page structure specifically for mobile, since most of the client's traffic
  came from phones.
- Handled deployment and hosting setup so the client didn't need to manage any infrastructure.

## Result

The rebuilt site loads noticeably faster on mobile and looks consistent across every page and
device. More importantly, the client's team now makes routine content updates themselves the
same day they're needed, instead of waiting on a developer for changes that used to take days.
