## Problem

A small business's existing website was built years ago on a slow, hard-to-update site builder,
making even small content changes require outside help and leaving the site noticeably slow on
mobile.

## Approach

Rebuilt the site as a fast, modern React application with a clean component structure, and set
up a simple content workflow so the client's team could update text and images themselves
without touching code.

## Result

The new site loads noticeably faster on mobile, looks consistent across devices, and the client
can now make routine content updates on their own instead of waiting on a developer.
