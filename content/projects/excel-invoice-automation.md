## Problem

A small logistics company was reconciling supplier invoices against delivery records by hand
every week, copying numbers between spreadsheets and manually flagging mismatches — a slow,
error-prone process for whoever was on invoice duty that week.

## Approach

Built a Power Query pipeline to pull in the invoice and delivery-record exports automatically,
added VBA macros to flag mismatches and generate a formatted summary sheet, and packaged it all
into a single workbook so anyone on the team could run it with one click.

## Result

What used to take about six hours a week of manual reconciliation now takes under 30 minutes,
and the automated mismatch flags have caught billing errors that were previously missed.
