## Problem

A regional retail client was spending several days each month manually compiling sales figures
from multiple store spreadsheets into a single report for leadership, with no way to drill into
trends between stores or product categories.

## Approach

Consolidated the store-level data into a single model, built DAX measures for month-over-month
and category-level comparisons, and designed a Power BI dashboard with drill-through pages for
regional managers to explore their own store's numbers without needing a new report every time.

## Result

The monthly reporting cycle dropped from about three days of manual work to a dashboard that
refreshes automatically each morning, and store managers can now self-serve the numbers they
need directly.
